Our Story

Driven by the Sea

MYA was founded to bring a truly world-class yachting experience to Western Australia. Combining the finest European boatbuilders with personal, deeply knowledgeable service — we believe ownership should be as effortless as the open water itself.

Headquartered on Fremantle's iconic waterfront and proud members of the DCH Marine Network, we represent two of the world's most celebrated marques and back them with 80+ years of combined marine expertise.

"We don't simply sell yachts. We deliver a lifetime on the water."— The MYA Team
