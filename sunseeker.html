Sunseeker Yachts

The world's leading brand for luxury motor yachts. Every Sunseeker is the result of an uncompromising approach to design and engineering — handcrafted in Britain, delivered to Fremantle.

The Range

Four Iconic Ranges

From the adrenaline of a Predator to the grand-tourer poise of the Ocean series, the Sunseeker range spans 55 to 134 feet. Each yacht is custom-commissioned and delivered by our Fremantle team.

Heritage & Innovation

Over Half a Century of
British Craftsmanship

When Robert Braithwaite started up the engine of a small open-cockpit speedboat in 1969, it wasn't just the Hostess 17 that roared into life — it was a new standard of yacht making. That spirit lives on in every Sunseeker built today.
